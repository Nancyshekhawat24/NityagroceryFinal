Top 5 Healthy Snacks for Kids

1. Apple Slices with Peanut Butter
Sweet, crunchy, and protein-packed!
Recipe:
- Slice 1 apple into wedges
- Spread 1 tsp peanut butter on each slice
- Optional: sprinkle cinnamon or granola on top
2. Yogurt with Berries
Creamy and rich in antioxidants.
Recipe:
- Add 1/2 cup Greek yogurt to a bowl
- Top with 1/4 cup fresh berries (blueberries, strawberries, etc.)
- Optional: drizzle a little honey or sprinkle chia seeds
3. Veggie Sticks and Hummus
Crunchy, colorful, and full of fiber!
Recipe:
- Slice carrots, cucumbers, and bell peppers into sticks
- Serve with 1/4 cup hummus in a small bowl
- Optional: sprinkle with paprika or lemon juice
4. Cheese Cubes with Whole Grain Crackers
A great combo of calcium and energy.
Recipe:
- Cut cheese (cheddar or mozzarella) into small cubes
- Arrange with 6–8 whole grain crackers
- Optional: add a few grapes for a sweet touch
5. Banana Oat Cookies
Soft, naturally sweet, and easy to make!
Recipe:
- Mash 2 ripe bananas in a bowl
- Mix with 1 cup rolled oats
- Optional: add raisins or chocolate chips
- Scoop onto baking tray and bake at 180°C (350°F) for 12–15 mins
